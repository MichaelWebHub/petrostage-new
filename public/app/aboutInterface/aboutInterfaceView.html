ABOUT

“PetroStage” is an initiative to overcome the communication barriers between international students. The project was launched by students of eight different universities (AGH Krakow, Poland; Alexandria University, Egypt; Gubkin University, Russia; Montanuniversität Leoben, Austria; RWTH Aachen University, Clausthal University of Technology, both Germany; University of Kansas, USA; University of Zagreb, Croatia) in November 2016 at the “Oil and Gas Horizons” Congress in Moscow (Russia).

One of the main goals of the project is to collect information about all international events, organized by and for students, in one place which will always be available for everybody. This is why “PetroStage” was created. We hope that “PetroStage” will expand networking opportunities for students and help organizers raise their events to a complete new level!

If your organization or university is organizing an international event (Conference, Congress, Forum, etc.) related to the petroleum industry or associated fields, please feel free to add it to our calendar! If you face any difficulties doing so, you can contact the author of this website, Michael Kutateladze from Gubkin University at michael.kutateladze@yandex.ru.

For any suggestions or more information about the project, please contact us via speconnectnow@gmail.com or visit our blog .
